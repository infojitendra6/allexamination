Voter ID Online Form

Name of Post: Voter ID Online Form
Post Date : 23 April 2019
Short description : National Voter Service Portal NVSP Are Invited to Online Application Form for the New Voter id Registration for Across India All People Those Are Eligible for this, This Portal Subscribers Can Any Request to Change / Update / Correction in Old Voter id, Search Your Name in Election Roll and Many Other Service.

Short Details of Application

Eligibility

  • Nationality : Indian
  • Place of Birth : India
  • Minimum Age : 18 Years

    • How to Apply New Form

    • Open New Voters ID Registration Form.
    • Enter Your Mobile No.
    • Enter Your OTP / SMS Password
    • Fill Your Basic Details
    • Upload Photo and Related Docs.
    • Submit Document to Concerned Authority

Correction in Voter ID

  • If you have Already Get the VOTER ID and Some Correction / Wrong Information I ID Card.
  • Open Correction Link Enter Your Mobile NO. and Get OTP Passed.
  • See Your Details and Change it If Required and Submit the Form.

Other Details of Voter ID

  • Check Your Electoral Roll / Know Your Both / Know Your BLO / ERO and DEO
  • If you have Already Enrolled / Voter ID and Checking Your Name in Electoral Roll / List Can Click on the Check Name in Election List Enter Your EPIC No. or Name, DOB, Father Name, State, ETC Details to Check Your Current Status.
